# cookd — Brand Identity ("HOT PRESS")

The complete brand system for cookd: colors, type, components, voice. Built for reuse in
social posts, swag, decks, and any design tool (Canva/Figma-ready — every value is exact).

**The brand in one line:** a smug tabloid that prints developers' AI-usage failures.
Loud headlines, physical receipts, rubber stamps, and a persona that is funny, witty,
brutal, and entirely full of itself.

---

## 1. Logo / wordmark

- **Wordmark:** `cookd.` — always lowercase, set in **Anton**, with the period in Flame
  (`#FF4D00`). The period is part of the mark; never omit it.
- **Long-form masthead:** `the cookd press` (Anton, lowercase) with the slogan in
  Fraunces italic: *“— all the flops fit to print”*.
- Clear space: leave at least the height of the “c” around the mark. Don’t outline,
  shadow, gradient, or tilt the wordmark (tilting is reserved for stamps).
- On dark, wordmark is Bone `#F2EAD9`; on light, Char `#181410`. Period stays Flame.

| Voice | Font | Weight / style | Case | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **THE HEADLINE (yelling)** | **Anton** | 400 (its only weight) | ALL CAPS | Headlines, stamps, hero numbers, buttons, wordmark. Tight leading 0.95–1.05, letter-spacing +0.01–0.06em |

## 4. Signature components & motifs

- **The ticker** — full-width scrolling banner. Flame bg + near-black text (breaking),
  Stamp Yellow bg (developing), Morgue Blue bg (closed). Items separated by `★`.
- **The rubber stamp** — Anton caps inside a 3–3.5px border, radius 5–6px, rotated
  ±7–12°, in Stamp Yellow or Flame (Morgue for cold). Always slams (scale 2.4→1 with
  overshoot). Canon stamps: `COOKED`, `CERTIFIED FLOP`, `PRESS CLOSED`, `EXCLUSIVE`,
  `FREQUENT FLYER`, `SPEEDRAN`, `ON THE COUCH`.
- **The receipt** — cream paper, perforated bottom edge (scalloped), dashed header rule,
  dotted row rules, mono text, right-aligned values; always ends in a TOTAL/VERDICT line.
  Titles like `THE DAMAGE`, `RUNNING TAB`, `LIFETIME RECORD`.
- **The front-page card** — card with 1.5px ink border + hard offset shadow (5–6px,
  no blur), masthead rule, Anton headline, Fraunces deck, **barcode footer** with mono
  stat caption. The shareable unit.
- **The barcode** — decorative stripes (2/1/3px bars), with stats printed beneath in mono.
- **The ticket stub** — two sections split by a dashed perforation; left = label + mono
  countdown, right = striped stub (`ADMIT ONE FLOP`, `NO REFUNDS`).
- **The mugshot** — avatar on a height-chart background (horizontal stripes), square frame
  with colored heat-ring border, name plate beneath (`Nº 0610-KNWR`).
- **Offense tags** — small bordered mono chips, slightly rotated alternately (±1.2°):
  `TOKEN ARSONIST`, `NIGHT OWL`, `CTX HOARDER`, `AGENT OVERLORD`, `GRASS: UNTOUCHED`.
- **Clippings** — saved cards rotated ±1.3° with translucent Stamp-Yellow tape strips.
- **Texture** — subtle film grain (≈14–16% opacity fractal noise, overlay blend) on all
  surfaces; halftone acceptable on posters.
- **Icons** — flame (reactions), ✎ (letters/comments), ✂ (clip/save), ↗ (syndicate/share).

## 5. Motion (for video/social)

Stamps **slam** (scale-down + slight rotate + settle, ~450ms, springy). Headlines tick in.
Receipts print downward. Heat arcs sweep with a glow bloom (~1.6s ease-out). Tickers
scroll continuously. Lock transition: everything cools to Morgue Blue, `PRESS CLOSED`
slams. One loud moment per scene; the chassis stays still.

## 6. Voice & copy (the persona IS the brand)

- The product speaks in first person as a smug tabloid editor. Funny, witty, brutal,
  full of itself. Roasts the **habit**, never the person; no slurs, no punching down.
- Numbers are always real and always shown — the receipts are the proof of the joke.
- Buttons are in on it: `RUIN ME`, `PRINT IT TO THE PAPER`, `SYNDICATE IT`,
  `PRESENT CREDENTIALS`, `SHARE THE SHAME`, `I’LL DO IT LATER (COWARD)`.
- States roast: loading = “reading your crimes…”; empty = “no flops yet. statistically
  suspicious.”; errors self-roast.
- Canon taglines:
  - *“the app you can only open when you’re cooked.”*
  - *“the daily paper of people who promptly ran out of prompts.”*
  - *“all the flops fit to print.”*
  - *“no terminal, no entry.”*
  - *“verified by usage, not vibes.”* (Engineer Card)
  - *“back to work, chef.”* (lock)
- Vocabulary: cooked/cooking, torched, the paper, letters to the editor, the pile-on,
  clippings, prints, the record, syndicate, field reporter (the companion), press code,
  byline, rap sheet, the editor.

## 7. Layout rules (posts/swag)

- High contrast: Char or Newsprint grounds only; cards carry the content.
- Hard offset shadows (4–6px solid, no blur) on cards; hairline borders elsewhere.
- Radii: cards 6px (newsprint feel) · app surfaces/tickets 10px · buttons 10–18px ·
  pills 99px. Stamps 5–6px.
- Generous margins; one headline dominates; stats live in a receipt or barcode footer;
  exactly one stamp per artifact.
- Share/export card ratio: 1080×1350 (4:5) with the `cookd.` watermark bottom-right and
  a barcode + stats strip.
- Swag-friendly one-color treatments: Flame-on-Char, Char-on-Newsprint, or Stamp-Yellow
  stamp motifs alone (the `COOKED` stamp works as a standalone print).

## 8. Don'ts

No purple-on-white gradients · no Inter/Roboto/Arial · no emoji storms (≤1 per artifact) ·
no blurred drop shadows on cards (offset only) · no tilting anything except stamps,
offense tags, and clippings · no flame-colored body text · never fake the numbers ·
never punch at the person.
